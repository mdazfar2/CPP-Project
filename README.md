## Description

Welcome to the Car Racing Game in C++! This exciting game allows you to step into the world of high-speed racing, where you can choose your dream car, compete against challenging opponents, and experience the thrill of the race.

## Features

- Realistic car physics for an immersive racing experience.
- A variety of high-performance cars to choose from, each with unique characteristics.
- Multiple challenging tracks and environments.
- AI opponents with adjustable difficulty levels.
- Power-ups and boosters to enhance your racing performance.
- Multiplayer mode for head-to-head competition.
- Stunning graphics and dynamic sound effects.
- Achievements and leaderboards to showcase your skills.
